About Green Core

Pioneering industrial paper manufacturing and automated supply logistics for over 15 years.

Our History

Founded in 2011, Green Core started as a regional recycling paper mill. Seeing the rapid growth of e-commerce packaging and the need for structural grade paper rolls with certified burst factors, we automated our slitter-winders and introduced digitized tracking systems.

Today, our plant processes over 120,000 metric tons of fiber annually. We serve wholesale packaging plants and pharmaceutical distributors globally, keeping all mechanical paper properties (moisture, tensile strength, and grammage) consistent through PLC-controlled sensors.
